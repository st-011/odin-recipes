Salmon

Description:

Pan fried Alaskan salmon with potatoes, asparagus, and cream sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. small gold potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Broccoli

Steps:

  1. Roast the potatoes
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Bake the salmon and broccoli
  4. You're done!
