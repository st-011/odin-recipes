Fried Chicken

Description:

Crispy Fried Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 3 teaspoons pepper
  • 2.5 teaspoons poultry seasoning
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 broiler/fryer chickens cut up
  • Oil for deep-fat frying

Steps:

  1. Combine flour, garlic salt, paprika, peper, and ooultry seasoning...(make breading)
  2. Fry until golden brown
