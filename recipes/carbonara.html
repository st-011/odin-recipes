Carbonara

Description:

Simple pasta carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp. kosher salt
  • 4 oz. guanciale
  • 2 oz. parmesan or pecorino romano
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 large eggs
  • Black pepper
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lp. spaghetti

Steps:

  1. Heat water and add salt
  2. Cut guanciale and grate cheese
  3. Whisk egg yolks, add cheese and pepper
  4. Fry guanciale
  5. Remove pot from heat
  6. Cook 1lb. pasta
  7. Add pasta to dutch oven
  8. Cook pasta in dutch oven
  9. Add egg mixture
  10. Mix in guanciale
